    About Us

    Shreeyash College of Engineering & Technology (SYCET) established in 2008, is one of the top Engineering colleges in India, and has been awarded by NAAC. SYCET is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. Recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and DTE and affiliated to the Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar Technical University, Lonere, Maharashtra.

    B.Tech (Under Graduate)

    B.Tech full form is Bachelor of Technology. It is one of the prestigious and most popular courses opted by science students. It is a 4-year full-time undergraduate course and is offered in various disciplines.

    M.Tech (Post Graduate)

    M. Tech full form is Macter of Technology. It is one of the prestigious and most popular courses opted by B.Tech students. It is a 2-year full-time postgraduate course and is offered in various disciplines.

    M.B.A.- Post Graduate Program

    M.B.A. full form is Master of Business Administration.It is a 2-year full-time postgraduate course.MBA courses prepare students for leadership roles in the business world and enable them to find work in varied industries.
